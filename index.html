Services

E-Commerce

We help business owners build Ecommerce applications integrated with payment gateways such as Stripe, Paypal and Mpesa.  Depending on the business needs, we either build custom full stack ecommerce web applications or use Ecommerce platforms such as Shopify and Woocommerce.

Custom Websites

Your website is your gateway to the digital marketing world. Whether you are a big company or a freelancer, having a good website that represents your brand, adds credibility to your business. Its no myth that having a good website is directly proportional to more leads and more sales. Let us build you an authentic custom website that represents your brand!

Digital Marketing and consulting

About

Let our story inspire you

  • 2017-2018

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We started back in 2017 as a digital marketing firm. We were committed to helping small scale business owners market their products online through face book ads and google ads. We helped a lot of small business owners reach more customers and grow their business. Times change and we have observed how the digital marketing world has been evolving since. We are therefore able to craft an efficient marketing strategy personalized for every business.  

  • 2019

    Web Development Services

    We realized that one of the common problems our clients were facing, was poorly designed websites. Others did not have a business website at all. We added a few software engineers to the team and since then, we have been able to develop close to a thousand efficient websites for our different clients.

  • December 2022

    Transition into a full software company

    We started building mobile applications and custom digital products targeted to specific industries. Some of the industries we have worked with include, finance, education and health.
